= Features =
* **Disable right click** on your blog 
* **Disable selection of text** on your blog

You must enable the features from options page to protect your blog.

== Installation ==

1. Upload `blog-protector-final.php` to the `/wp-content/plugins/` directory
2. Activate the plugin through the 'Plugins' menu in WordPress
3. Go to **Settings** and then **Blog Protector Final** to configure and protect your blog.
